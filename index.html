About me

Hello I am Albert.
A Canadian, living and studying in Hong Kong.

I am currently a Ph.D. canadiate in Physics,
in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

I enjoy the night sky since I was a child, and I keep wondering how the universe works.
Because of that I decided to pursue my career in astronomy research.

Curriculum Vitae

Researches

Research Interest:

Instrumental and Experimental Astronomy, especially on Photon Detecting Instruments and mixed signal (analog and digitial) Readout System Development.

Current project: Single Photon Imagier for Nanosecond Astrophysics (SPINA), supervised by Prof. George Smoot.

Research Highlights

Start-up: Centauri Optics

In year 2018 (just before I started my Ph.D.), I found a start-up company with
Prof. KS Wong (my undergraduate supervisor), Dr. Chan and Anthony Lam.

We aim to develop a low-cost, portable and remote controllable microscope
for laboratory and educational usage.
